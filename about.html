About Mrs. Davis

Explore


Mrs. Elizabeth Davis ... I am a member of the Fightin' Texas Aggie Class of 1988 and a Magna Cum Laude graduate with a B.S. in Curriculum and Instruction - English Composite teaching certification. After teaching senior English, on-level and Advanced Placement, and sponsoring the yearbook for fifteen years at A & M Consolidated High School, my family moved to Austin twelve years ago. I have enjoyed teaching junior on-level, junior Advanced Placement and senior Advanced Placement English at Westwood. This will begin my 13th year as a Warrior. Once again this year, I will be teaching my dream schedule, both AP English Language and Composition (AP English III) and AP Literature and Composition (AP English IV).
In my 27-year career, a number of incredible experiences have shaped my teaching skills and practices, including teaching Saturday student sessions for SAT and AP and even working with the Texas A & M Medical School teaching MCAT prep in a summer program. In addition, for almost 20 years, I have been lucky enough to serve as a consultant for The College Board, teaching conferences, leading summer institutes, presenting student prep sessions, and mentoring teachers. Every workshop I present resonates in my own classroom as I gain knowledge and skills from networking with other teachers and developing new methods and ideas.
When I am not grading papers, I love spending time with my family, husband Mickey (Mineral and Gas appraiser for Capital Appraisal Group), son Josh (Graduate student at the University of Oklahoma), daughter-in-law Kaitlyn (professional counselor), son Nick and future daughter-in-law Hannah (seniors at Texas A & M University). I sing soprano in my church choir and am involved in the women's ministry on occasion. For the last few years, I have completely spoiled my "empty nest" puppy named "Aggie May" and my old dog "Buster."
