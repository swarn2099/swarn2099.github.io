What are we About?

We are the RoboWarriors. We like to take on any challenge, big or small, with our war face on. There isn't any obstacle, any hurdle, or any depth we won't cross or swim too. Our club of 50 members boast a variety of skills. Engineering, design, communication, and buisness are just a few of the section we assort our members in. All our skillsets vary in category and magnitude. That's our greatest strength. When the time comes, we unite to help the strong become stronger, the new to become experienced. This helps propel our club forward to achieve wonders.


The Concept

Farming is essential to our lives.


Where would we be without food? What state would our planet be if all our agricultural land were shopping malls and pollution-creating factories?

In a world full of many distractions and new innovations, we tend to forget our achievemnts from the past. As engineers and innovators, we like to improve like to see the evolution of old into new and there is no better example of this phenomenan than agriculture and cultivation. We have been farming for millions of years and have improved with the process through the use of tools. Lets improve it further with the use of robotics and science.
