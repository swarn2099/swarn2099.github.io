About Juhi

Juhi Kumar

Juhi has been dancing since the age of 3. She has been trained in the Indian classical dance: Bharatanatyam for 9 years, Ballet for 4 years, and was on her high school dance team, Warrior Pride, for 3 years where she was trained in Jazz, Hip Hop, Contemporary, Tap, Ballet. Juhi performed in Austinâ€™s first Bollywood Musical: Om Shanti: Once Upon A Time in Bollywood, as the youngest dancer. Juhi has been teaching at Monsoon Dance for 3 years as a dance instructor and she has made the move from Austin, Texas to Dallas, Texas where she currently attends the University of Texas at Dallas. At UTD, Juhi is a dancer on the Sharara Competitive Bollywood Dance Team.
