Add an Event

Start by filling out the event cards with your event's basic information such as name, description, category, and more. Once you fill out the add page, click submit and view a card preview for a final check. Once you click submit, you'll have 24 hours to edit the event until a Traveler approves it for the app.
edit

Manage your Events

After submiting the event, you have 24 hours to make final changes which can be done through the Manage page. Edit all the things you added and reupload the card for review.

See it on marcopolo

Once you submit an event, a campus Traveler will approve the event and then you'll see your event on the app!
